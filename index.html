This is the current Nym's full pubkey. A "pubkey" aka "public key" is a part of a keypair in cryptography. A keypair is two connected codes: your public key (like a username that identifies you to everyone) and your private key (like a password that only you should know and use to prove you're really you). You use this pubkey for others to find you on Nymchat and you use other people's pubkeys to find them.
